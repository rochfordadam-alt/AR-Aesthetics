Aftercare

Anti-wrinkle aftercare

  • Don’t rub or massage for 24h
  • Avoid hard exercise, alcohol, sauna for 24h
  • Effects begin 3–14 days; last ~3–4 months
  • Attend your 2-week review

Dermal filler aftercare

  • No make-up on area for 12h
  • Redness/swelling/lumps settle in 1–2 weeks
  • Avoid hard exercise, alcohol, sauna for 24h
  • Urgent: severe pain, pale/blue skin, vision changes
