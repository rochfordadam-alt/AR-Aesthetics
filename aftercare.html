Aftercare

Anti-wrinkle aftercare

  • Do not rub or massage treated areas for 24 hours.
  • Avoid strenuous exercise, alcohol and sauna for 24 hours.
  • Effects begin 3–14 days and last ~3–4 months.
  • Attend your two-week review.

Dermal filler aftercare

  • No make-up on the area for 12 hours.
  • Redness/swelling/lumps usually settle within 1–2 weeks.
  • Avoid strenuous exercise, alcohol and sauna for 24 hours.
  • Urgent: severe pain, pale/blue skin, vision changes → contact us or 999.
