Privacy Notice

We respect your privacy and handle your information in line with UK GDPR and healthcare standards.

Who we are

Renew Clinic (Bath) is owned and operated by Adam Rochford (HCPC-registered Physiotherapist and Independent Prescriber). Contact: info@renewclinic.uk.

What we collect & why

  • Identification & contact details: to arrange appointments and communicate with you.
  • Medical information & photos: to assess suitability, prescribe appropriately, and maintain safe clinical records.
  • Consent & treatment records: to evidence decision-making and provide aftercare.

Lawful basis

We process personal data under legitimate interests (service delivery), contract (appointments), consent (marketing if opted-in), and for medical/healthcare purposes with appropriate safeguards.

How we store and share data

  • Records are stored securely and retained in line with clinical guidance.
  • We only share data where necessary for your care (e.g., pharmacy for named-patient prescriptions) or where required by law.
  • We do not sell personal data.

Your rights

  • Access, correction, deletion (where applicable), restriction and objection.
  • Withdraw marketing consent at any time.
  • Complain to the ICO if you’re unhappy: ico.org.uk.

Contact

Questions or requests about your data: info@renewclinic.uk.
