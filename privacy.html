Privacy & GDPR

How AR Aesthetics collects, uses and protects your personal data in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

Data Controller: AR Aesthetics (Bath, UK). Contact: rochford.adam@gmail.com

What we collect

Identity & contact details, medical history & medication (for safe treatment), photos (with consent), appointment records.

Lawful basis

  • Provision of care (Art 9(2)(h))
  • Contract/Legitimate interests for bookings
  • Consent for marketing/photos

How we use data

To assess suitability, deliver treatment, provide aftercare, arrange reviews, and manage safety. We never sell your data.

Storage & retention

Records retained for 7 years unless law/insurers require longer.

Sharing

We may share with your GP or emergency services for safety, or with insurers/regulators if required by law.

Your rights

Request access, correction, deletion, restriction or portability; object to processing; withdraw consent anytime.

Cookies

Only essential cookies by default. If analytics are added later, we will ask for consent.

Concerns? See the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
