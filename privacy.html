Privacy Notice (UK GDPR)

This notice explains how AR Aesthetics collects, uses and protects your personal data in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

Data Controller: AR Aesthetics (Bath, UK). Contact: rochford.adam@gmail.com

What we collect

Identity & contact details, medical history and medication (for safe treatment), photos (with consent), and appointment records.

Lawful basis

  • Provision of care (Article 9(2)(h)) for health data necessary for treatment
  • Contract/Legitimate interests for bookings and communications
  • Consent for marketing and for using photos

How we use data

To assess suitability, deliver treatment, provide aftercare, arrange reviews, and manage safety. We never sell your data.

Storage & retention

Records are stored securely and retained for 7 years (adults) unless a longer period is required by law or insurers.

Sharing

We may share information with your GP or emergency services where necessary for safety, or with insurers/regulators if legally required.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, restriction or portability of your data. You may object to processing and can withdraw consent at any time.

Cookies & analytics

We use only essential cookies by default. If we add analytics in future, we will ask for consent and update this notice.

If you have concerns, you can contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
