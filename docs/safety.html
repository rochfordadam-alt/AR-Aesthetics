Safety, Risks & Aftercare

Clear information to help you decide, prepare and recover well. Includes risks, expected side-effects and when to seek urgent help.

Anti-wrinkle (Botulinum toxin)

Readable, patient-friendly guidance and evidence-based risks.

Download: Botox Aftercare & Risks (.docx)

Key points:

  • Results develop in 3–14 days; last ~3–4 months.
  • Headache ~9%; eyelid droop ~3% (glabella), brow droop ~2% (forehead).
  • Avoid rubbing/exercise for 24h. Contact us urgently for severe headache, vision or swallowing issues.

Hyaluronic Acid Dermal Filler

Aftercare, risks and rare complications with incidence ranges.

Download: Filler Aftercare & Risks (.docx)

Key points:

  • Swelling 24–72h (lips up to 5 days). Bruising common.
  • Vascular occlusion rare (~0.01%–0.05%); urgent review same day if severe pain/colour change.
  • Vision change after nose/glabella work → call 999 and contact us immediately.

Urgent concerns

If you experience severe pain, sudden colour change (pale/grey/mottled), blistering, or any vision change, contact us immediately. In an emergency, call 999.
