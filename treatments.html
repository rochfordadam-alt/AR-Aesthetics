Treatments

Focused, safe treatments delivered by an HCPC-registered Independent Prescriber. Every appointment includes a medical assessment and personalised aftercare.

Wrinkle-Relaxing (Botulinum Toxin)

Upper Face Now available

  • Glabella (frown lines)
  • Frontalis (forehead lines)
  • Crow’s feet (smile lines around eyes)

Natural softening of movement while keeping expression. Full effect by 10–14 days; typical longevity 3–4 months.

Brands: Azzalure® / Bocouture® (medical grade).

Teeth Grinding / Jawline Slimming (Masseter)

Targeted injections into the masseter muscle can reduce clenching/grinding, jaw ache and wideness. Results build over 4–6 weeks; may also soften the jawline.

Note: Offered following individual assessment. If this is new for you clinically, list as “available on request / limited availability” until you’re ready to offer routinely.

Underarm Sweating (Hyperhidrosis)

Injections reduce sweat activity for 4–6+ months in most people.

Availability: If this is not yet in your scope, keep this paragraph and mark as “coming soon”.

Dermal Fillers (Hyaluronic Acid)

Nasolabial Folds

Softens the “smile lines” running from nose to mouth, restoring gentle support while keeping natural expression.

Marionette Lines

Fills shadowing at the corners of the mouth to reduce a “downturned” look.

Lip Fillers

Shape, hydration and balanced volume using CE-marked HA fillers. Options from subtle border definition to fuller plump.

Dissolving (hyaluronidase) available where clinically indicated.
