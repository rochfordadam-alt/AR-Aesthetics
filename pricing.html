How many units do I need for Botox?

Each area typically requires 10–25 units depending on strength of muscle activity and gender. This is assessed individually at consultation — we focus on natural, balanced results rather than fixed “packages.”

When will I see results?

Botulinum toxin results begin around day 3–5 and reach full effect by 14 days. Dermal filler results are immediate but settle further over 2–3 weeks as swelling reduces.

Do I need to come back for review?

Yes — follow-up at 2–3 weeks ensures safety and satisfaction. Adjustments are made only if clinically appropriate, and are included in your treatment cost.
