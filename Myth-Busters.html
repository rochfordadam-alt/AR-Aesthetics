Myth Busters

Straight answers to common questions so you can make informed choices.

“Botox freezes your face.”

It shouldn’t. Appropriate dosing softens movement while keeping expression. The goal is natural—not frozen.

“Fillers always look obvious.”

Poor technique and overfilling look obvious. Conservative, anatomically-aware placement should look like you, just fresher.

“Once you start, you can’t stop.”

You can stop any time. Results gradually wear off; you’ll return to baseline. No rebound “worse ageing.”

“Underarm injections are only for severe cases.”

They’re for anyone significantly bothered by underarm sweating after simple measures. Effects commonly last 4–6+ months.

“Teeth grinding injections weaken the jaw permanently.”

They temporarily relax the masseter muscles. Chewing adapts; comfort usually improves in 4–6 weeks. Effects wear off over months.

“Bruising means something went wrong.”

Not necessarily—small bruises are common and self-limit. We’ll advise if anything needs review.

“There’s no regulation.”

Treatments are prescribed medicines in the UK. I’m HCPC-registered and follow medical governance with named-patient prescriptions and pharmacy supply.
