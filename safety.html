Safety & Aftercare

Clinical, patient-centred care with clear guidance and a built-in review. Please read the relevant aftercare below.

Anti-wrinkle Aftercare (Upper Face, Masseter, Underarms)

  • Make-up: avoid over treated areas for 4 hours. No rubbing/massage that time.
  • Exercise/sauna/alcohol: avoid for the rest of the day.
  • Sleep: try not to lie face-down the first night.
  • Onset & duration: effect builds over 3–7 days (full by ~14 days). Typical duration 3–4 months.
  • Review: included at 2–3 weeks. Further dosing only if clinically indicated for the agreed plan.
  • Masseter: results for clenching build over 4–6 weeks.
  • Underarms: reduced sweating may last 4–6+ months.

Dermal Filler Aftercare (Lips, Nasolabial, Marionette)

  • Swelling/bruising: common for 24–72h (lips can take longer). Cold compresses in short intervals can help.
  • Avoid: strenuous exercise, saunas, alcohol on the day; no dental work for 2 weeks unless urgent.
  • Make-up: avoid over injection points for 24h.
  • Lumps: gentle fingertip smoothing after 48–72h only if advised.
  • Call urgently if you notice: severe pain, dusky/white skin, increasing mottled discolouration, or visual symptoms.

Risks – Summary

We discuss personalised risks during consultation.

  • Anti-wrinkle: headache, mild bruising; temporary eyelid/eyebrow heaviness (usually rare and self-limiting); asymmetry may need adjustment.
  • Masseter: temporary chewing fatigue, smile change (usually settles).
  • Underarm: local tenderness, minor bruising.
  • Filler: swelling/bruising, asymmetry/irregularity, infection (rare), delayed swelling; vascular compromise is rare but serious — we are trained and stocked for emergency management.

Emergency support information is provided on your aftercare sheet. If worried, contact us promptly.
