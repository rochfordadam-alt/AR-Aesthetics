Safety, Risks & Aftercare

At Renew Aesthetics & Wellbeing, your safety and comfort are our top priorities. All injectable treatments are carried out by an HCPC-registered Independent Prescriber, following current aesthetic medicine safety guidelines. Every patient receives a full medical assessment before treatment.
